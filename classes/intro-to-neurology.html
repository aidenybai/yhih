Intro to Neurology · Exploring the nervous system

In this class, we will be exploring the most complex and unique system in body: the nervous system. We will be striving to learn what the nervous system does and how it does it. We will cover the anatomy and function of the nervous system, as well as disease and issues that can arise in the nervous system. If you plan on taking AP Biology, AP Psychology, or just want to understand how the body and nervous system works, this is the class for you.
