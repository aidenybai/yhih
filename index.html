Mission

Youth Hand in Hand is a nonprofit organization committed to giving all students an equal opportunity to learn and succeed, both inside and outside school. We believe that we can lessen this gap through free online classes, with people to guide them along every step of the way.

All of our profits will go to aiding the local youth, providing them with more educational opportunities!
